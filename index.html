Get Started

Infinite Scope ♾️

Aerowave can be installed very easily where any sort of transaction mechanism is being followed, be it in Ticket Counters, ATM's, Airports etc.

Easy to use ⚡

Deployment requires a system with minimum 4gb of RAM integrated with a webcam and a R-pi, rest our software take care of everything :)

Features

Explore the various features that Aerowave provides ✨

Gesture Controlled

Touchless 🙅‍♂️

Offering seamless convenience, cutting out the middleman as you order your burger, check in for your flight, or get cash out at the ATM.

Gesture Keyboard

Customizable ⚙️

Personalize your custom gestures to interact with the Keyboard touchlessly from as far as 7 feets ensuring you're safe from areal contamination too!

Contact Tracing

Secured 🛡️

We keep a realtime track of those who doesn't wear masks to provide a contact-tracing data log which can be used for tracking.

Speech Interaction

Talk 🔊

Aerowave supports S2S (Speech to Speech) interaction integrated with few customizable operations manually controlled via gestures.
